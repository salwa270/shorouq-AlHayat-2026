üìù How to Write a CV (Resume)

A CV tells employers about you. Here is a simple way to make one:

  1. Write your name and contact info clearly at the top.
  2. List your skills (like "good listener", "punctual", "hard worker").
  3. Add your education (school names and dates).
  4. Ask a friend to read it and check for mistakes.

ü§ù Interview Tips

Meeting an employer can be nervous, but you can do it!

  1. Dress neatly. Wear clean clothes that make you feel good.
  2. Arrive early. Aim to be 10 minutes early.
  3. Smile and say hello. Being friendly is a great skill.
  4. Listen carefully to the questions. It's okay to take time to answer.
